About

Founded in 2019, Mayur Electricals has been providing top-notch electrical services for over 4 years. Starting as a small business, we've grown into a trusted name in the industry, known for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. We specialize in a wide range of electrical services, including residential, commercial, and industrial projects. From wiring and lighting to complex installations and maintenance, we ensure every job is done right the first time. With decades of experience, our team is equipped to handle any electrical challenge. We stay up-to-date with the latest technologies and industry standards to provide our clients with the best solutions possible.
